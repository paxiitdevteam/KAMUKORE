# Video Placeholders

This directory is ready for video assets.

## Placeholder Structure

Create placeholder videos for:
- Experience showcases
- Product demonstrations
- Cultural content
- Promotional videos

## Recommended Specifications

- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or 1280x720 (HD)
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Bitrate: 5-10 Mbps for Full HD
- Audio: AAC codec, 128-192 kbps

## File Naming

Use descriptive names:
- experience-craft-workshop.mp4
- product-demonstration.mp4
- cultural-tradition.mp4

## Multiple Formats

Provide multiple formats for compatibility:
- Primary: .mp4 (H.264)
- Secondary: .webm (VP9)
- Optional: .ogg (Theora)
